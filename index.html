Football Flashing News

Football Flashing News is an automated content service that publishes football (soccer) news — transfer updates, match results, and fixture previews — to its own operator's social media accounts on Instagram and TikTok.

It uses the official TikTok Content Posting API to publish video and image content to a single authorized TikTok account owned by the service operator.
