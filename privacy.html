Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Football Flashing News service ("the Service", "we", "us") handles data in connection with its integration with TikTok. The Service is an automated tool that publishes football (soccer) news content to a single TikTok account owned and authorized by the Service operator.

1. Data We Access

When the operator authorizes the Service through TikTok's official OAuth (Login Kit) flow, the Service receives:

  • Basic account information (user.info.basic) — the authorized account's TikTok open ID, display name, and avatar, used only to confirm the correct account is connected.
  • Content posting access (video.publish, video.upload) — permission to publish and upload the operator's own video reels and photo carousels to the authorized account. Video is sent by direct file upload; photo carousels are published from a public, ownership-verified HTTPS URL that TikTok downloads server-side.

The Service does not request, access, or store data belonging to any other TikTok user, and does not read followers, messages, comments, or private content.

2. How We Use Data

Account information and access tokens are used exclusively to publish the operator's own football-news content to the operator's own authorized TikTok account. We do not use the data for advertising, profiling, resale, or any purpose unrelated to publishing that content.

3. Data Storage and Security

OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored locally on the operator's own machine, in a file that is excluded from version control, and are used only to maintain the posting connection. Tokens are never transmitted to third parties. No end-user databases are maintained.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any TikTok data with third parties. Content is transmitted only to TikTok itself, through TikTok's official APIs, for the purpose of publishing it.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as the integration is active, and are deleted if the operator revokes authorization or removes the stored session file. Published posts are governed thereafter by TikTok's own data policies.

6. Your Rights and Revocation

The operator may revoke the Service's access at any time from their TikTok account settings (Security & permissions → Manage app permissions), which immediately invalidates the stored tokens.

7. Third-Party Policies

This integration relies on TikTok. Please also review TikTok's Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time; the "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact
